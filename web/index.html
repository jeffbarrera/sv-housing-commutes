In search of cheaper housing,
Silicon Valley workers face long commutes

As rents skyrocket across Silicon Valley, a growing number of workers have been priced out of job centers like Mountain View and Palo Alto.

Over the past five years, places with cheaper rents and lower median incomes have grown faster than the more affluent areas of Santa Clara and San Mateo counties.

Yet this shift away from expensive tech hubs comes at the cost of longer commutes, as workers have to drive further to get to their jobs.
